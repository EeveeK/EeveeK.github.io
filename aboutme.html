About Me

  Hello, I’m Kiera Kushnerus, a multifaceted enthusiast with a profound love for the arts. Whether I’m belting out tunes as a singer or playing glistening glissandos with my clarinet, music has always been an important part of my life and I play in my school’s top band and I am the most experienced singer in my choir. My journey also extends into the world of visual art, where I’m dedicated to honing my skills, continually seeking avenues for creative growth. Just in the past year I have made great strides in my artistic abilities and hope to see further growth in the future.

  Beyond the realm of arts, I’m captivated by the digital landscape. Excitingly, I’m gearing up for an adventure at Miami University, where I’ll immerse myself in the intricate craft of video game development. This passion converges with my commitment to academic excellence, as I navigate college-level computer science courses. I believe strongly that computer science skills are integral to not only my future as a game designer but also the world at large.

  What sparked my interest in video game design was naturally an interest in playing video games themselves. I believe video games are the pinnacle of creative works. Every one of my interests feeds into video games in a major way. All weaved together to create something full of passion. I once got the opportunity to playtest up and coming indie games in my area and I truly felt in my element. The people there had a strong dedication to their craft that I greatly admired. I hope that one day I can count myself among them and create something worthy of their and the wider public’s care.
